Program

This year we are delighted to invite Gen Kelsang Rigpa, the Western US Spiritual director for the NKT-IKBU to visit the Northwest and offer Medicine Buddha empowerment and teachings at the Northwest Dharma Celebration in Seattle.

We will have the opportunity to receive the blessings of Medicine Buddha and learn how to put these ancient instructions into practice. Gen Rigpa will teach us how to rely upon Medicine Buddha and heal ourself and others from pain and suffering.

The Celebration will be held at KMC Washington – a Kadampa Temple in Seattle dedicated to World Peace which arose from the vision of Venerable Geshe Kelsang Gyatso, the Founder of the New Kadampa Tradition, as part of the International Temples Project (ITP).
