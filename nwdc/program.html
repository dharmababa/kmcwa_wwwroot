Program

This year we are delighted to invite Gen Kelsang Rigpa, the Western US Spiritual director for the NKT-IKBU to visit the Northwest and offer Maitreya empowerment and teachings at the Northwest Dharma Celebration in Seattle.

We will have the opportunity to receive the blessings of Maitreya and learn how to put these ancient instructions into practice. Gen Rigpa will teach us how to rely upon Prajnaparamita and increase our ability to care for and love others, which is the source of all our happiness.

The Celebration will be held at KMC Washington – a Kadampa Temple in Seattle dedicated to World Peace which arose from the vision of Venerable Geshe Kelsang Gyatso, the Founder of the New Kadampa Tradition, as part of the International Temples Project (ITP).
